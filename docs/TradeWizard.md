# **The Problem: Why Prediction Markets Still Fail Most Traders**

Prediction markets are one of the most powerful tools ever created for forecasting real-world events. In theory, they aggregate collective intelligence into accurate probability signals.

In practice, however, most prediction market platforms suffer from a critical flaw:

**They provide markets — but no intelligence.**

Today’s prediction market users are expected to:

* Monitor breaking political news in real time  
* Understand polling methodology and statistical bias  
* Track sentiment shifts across media and social platforms  
* Interpret market order books and liquidity signals  
* Time entries and exits correctly

This creates a steep learning curve that turns prediction trading into a game dominated by:

* Political insiders  
* Quantitative traders  
* Information arbitrage professionals

For everyday users, prediction markets often feel like:

* Blind speculation  
* News-driven gambling  
* Emotional decision-making

As a result:

* Most traders lack confidence in their positions  
* Many abandon platforms after early losses  
* Market participation stays niche instead of going mainstream

Despite having access to the same markets, most users are trading without the analytical tools professionals rely on.

**Prediction markets today are missing the most important layer: intelligence.**

And that is exactly the gap TradeWizard is built to solve.

# **Regulatory Strategy & Risk Management**

TradeWizard is designed from day one to operate within regulated prediction market infrastructure while maintaining a clear separation between market execution and intelligence delivery.  
The platform’s regulatory posture is built on a simple principle:  
**TradeWizard provides intelligence. Regulated partners provide the trading rails.**  
This structure enables rapid scaling, institutional trust, and long-term defensibility.  
---

## **Regulatory Framework**

### **Regulated Market Infrastructure (Polymarket Integration)**

TradeWizard integrates with Polymarket’s regulated prediction market platform, which operates under the oversight of the U.S. Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC).  
Through Polymarket’s Builders Program, TradeWizard accesses:

* Regulated event contracts  
* KYC/AML-compliant onboarding  
* Custody, clearing, and settlement  
* Market surveillance and abuse monitoring  
* Contract approval and resolution processes

This ensures that all real-money trading activity occurs within a compliant, regulated environment.  
TradeWizard itself does not:

* Custody user funds  
* Clear trades  
* Settle contracts  
* Operate markets

It acts purely as an intelligence and decision-support layer.  
---

## **Intelligence Platform Classification**

TradeWizard is positioned as:

* A financial analytics platform

* A trading research and strategy tool

It does not act as:

* A broker-dealer
